The Artist

Hello, I'm TPR.

"Photography is the art of freezing time."

Based in Vijayawada, my journey began with a simple curiosity for light and shadow. What started as a hobby has evolved into a passionate pursuit of capturing raw, unfiltered human emotions. With 15+ years of experience, I've had the privilege of documenting countless beautiful moments.

Whether it's the grandeur of an Indian wedding or the quiet intimacy of a solo portrait, I approach every shoot with the same philosophy: Keep it real, make it cinematic.

When I'm not behind the lens, I'm usually scouting for new locations, color-grading my latest film, or designing the perfect album layout.
